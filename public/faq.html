Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a file?

Log in, navigate to the Upload page, and fill out the form with your file and details.

Go to the Current Folder page after logging in to see all posts, including yours.

Can I change my password?

Yes, visit the Settings page to update your password, email, or avatar.
